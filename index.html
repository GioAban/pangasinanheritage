Nature & Adventure

Hundred Islands

Hundred Islands is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Pangasinan. It features over a hundred small islands scattered across the sea, where visitors can enjoy island hopping, swimming, snorkeling, kayaking, and relaxing on white sand beaches with scenic ocean views.

Historical Landmark

Bolinao Lighthouse

Bolinao Lighthouse is a historic landmark built during the American period. It stands on a hill overlooking the West Philippine Sea, offering breathtaking panoramic views, cool sea breeze, and a peaceful atmosphere perfect for sightseeing and photography.

Discover More →
Wellness & Eco-Park

Balungao Hot Spring

Balungao Hot Spring is a relaxing eco-park destination located at the foot of Mt. Balungao. It is known for its naturally warm and therapeutic waters, making it ideal for relaxation, family outings, and visitors who want to enjoy nature and wellness activities.
