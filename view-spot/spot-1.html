Hundred Islands

Nature & AdventureAlaminos City

Overview

Hundred Islands is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Pangasinan. It features over a hundred small islands scattered across the sea, where visitors can enjoy island hopping, swimming, snorkeling, kayaking, and relaxing on white sand beaches with scenic ocean views.
